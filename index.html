Home

Did you know that according to the World Health Organization, “most of the world’s population live in countries where overweight and obesity kills more people than underweight”? The problem affects not only adults, but more and more children also achieve weight, which classifies their Body Mass Index (BMI) as overweight or obese. Aftermaths of the excessive body mass include non-communicable diseases like diabetes, cardiovascular disease, hypertension, and even some cancers. Being overweight or obese also shortens your life expectancy. The problem is even more severe in the global pandemic era. The good news is that most of these problems can be easily avoided if proper nutritional habits are being implemented. But the key is to follow good practices from a young age.
That’s why we invite you to join our EAT Nutriwise family and start the change today :)

Our aim

To raise awareness of actions and choices that lead (guide) to general well-being and preventing diet-induced diseases.

Our goal is to educate young people about healthy lifestyle habits, including proper diet, adequate physical activity level and hygienic circadian rhythm. We want to help whole families implement the change into their everyday lives and emphasize the importance of eating together - (both for health benefits and strengthening their bonds).

Content areas:

  1. Relationship with food / Food & emotions - eating as fulfilling the physiological need and showing the importance of sharing meals with family and friends.
  2. Balanced diet and lifestyle - considers combining groups of food into healthy meals and explaining how different nutrients influence our bodies and performance.
  3. Products and good choices - looks for smart shopping strategies and decisions, learns how to become a conscious consumer
  4. Increasing children’s culinary competences

Classes

Our program includes monthly meetings (stationary or online), cooking assignments, work cards, and if needed, dietary consultations.

During our meetings, we will talk about various aspects of nutrition - from the basics, like food pyramid and proper composition of meals, to subjects connected with a generally healthy lifestyle, like nutri-immunity or adequate physical activity.

  • Food pyramid - which groups of food should you choose and why?
  • Nutri-immunity - diet for your health
  • Healthy plate - how to balance your meals and avoid health problems in adult life
  • Labels - the key to a healthy diet
  • Staying fit in the pandemic era
  • Sugar - friend or foe? Healthy alternatives
  • Sensory eating - befriend your food
  • Water - queen of drinks
  • Lifestyle medicine - your superpower
  • Trends in diets - do popular diets make any sense?
